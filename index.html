📋 Interaction Guide

1. KEYBOARD → Deadline Essay Panic 📄

"When I type, my essay reacts — like a student's brain during a midnight deadline."

  • Fast + steady typing → 😌 In the Zone
  • No typing 5+ sec → 👀 Professor is Watching
  • Heavy backspacing → 😫 Existential Crisis
  • Very fast typing + errors → 🔥 Copium Mode

2. MOUSE → Cat & Laser Pointer 🐱

"When I move my mouse, the cat reacts — like a cat that has completely lost its mind over a laser dot."

  • Moving without clicking → 😻 cat pounces and chases the dot
  • Idle 10+ sec → 😴 cat gives up and curls up for a nap
  • Fast clicking → 🙀 cat goes full zoomies

3. SCROLL → Endless Dance Floor 🕺

"When I scroll, the dancer reacts — like flipping through a night out that never ends."

  • Fast continuous scroll → the dancer spins into a frenzy, lights flare up
  • Slow scroll/pause → "the DJ takes a breather 🎧"
  • Reach the bottom → 🎤 "Mic drop. Show's over."

4. MICROPHONE → Exam Hall Silence 🤫

"When I make noise, the invigilator reacts — like a strict exam proctor scanning the room."

  • Talking/loud sound → 👀 proctor turns, suspicion meter rises
  • Silence → proctor relaxes
  • Sudden loud sound → 🚨 "SUSPICIOUS ACTIVITY DETECTED"

5. PHONE MOTION → Sleepy Bus Commuter 😴

"When I tilt my phone, the commuter reacts — like falling asleep on a bus and jerking awake at your stop."

  • Slow forward tilt → head nods off
  • Sudden upright tilt → 😳 "MY STOP!"
  • Held flat/still → peaceful zzz bubbles

6. CAMERA → Online Class Attention Check 💻

"When I look away from the camera, the teacher reacts — like a professor calling you out on Zoom."

  • Facing camera → teacher calm, teaching
  • Turning away/leaving frame → 😳 "Are you listening?"
  • Absent too long → "ATTENDANCE MARKED ABSENT"
